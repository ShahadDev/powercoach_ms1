Powerlifting - What is it?

It is a form of competitive weightlifting in which contestants attempt three types of lift in a set sequence.

Bench

The most technical of the 3 lifts, involves a strong torson and leg drive

Squat

The riskiest, without proper form you are inevitably going to injured or fry your nervous system! Let us handle your recovery!

Deadlift

Sumo or Conventional! It's all the same, requires a good level of technique! The best is always saved till last
